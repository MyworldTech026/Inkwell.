About Inkwell

Inkwell.

Where developers and creators share ideas that matter.

What is Inkwell?

Inkwell is a free multi-user blogging platform built specifically for developers, students and creators who want to share knowledge, write articles and grow together as a community.

Whether you are a beginner who just wrote your first Hello World or a senior developer with years of experience — Inkwell is for you.

What you can do on Inkwell

  • ✍️ Write and publish articles on any topic
  • 📖 Read and discover posts from other writers
  • 🤍 Like posts and comments that helped you
  • 💬 Comment and engage with the community
  • 👥 Follow writers whose content you love
  • 📊 Track your views, likes and followers

Built by

Inkwell was designed and built by Muhammed Zulikaneni Omeiza — a self-taught frontend developer from Ilorin, Nigeria.

Built with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. No frameworks. Just consistency and passion. 🇳🇬

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Inkwell. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it and your rights regarding your data.

By using Inkwell you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you create an account on Inkwell we collect:

  • Your name and display name
  • Your email address
  • Your profile picture (if provided)
  • Your username
  • Content you publish including posts and comments
  • Your interactions such as likes and follows

If you sign in with Google we receive your name, email address and profile picture from your Google account.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your Inkwell account
  • Display your profile and published content
  • Enable you to interact with other users
  • Send you account related emails when necessary
  • Improve the Inkwell platform and user experience

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase — a platform provided by Google LLC. Firebase uses industry standard security measures to protect your information.

Your account authentication is managed by Firebase Authentication. Your posts, comments and profile information are stored in Firebase Firestore.

5. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade or share your personal information with third parties.

Content you publish on Inkwell such as posts and comments is visible to other users of the platform as that is the nature of a blogging platform.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Update or correct your profile information at any time
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Request information about how your data is used

To delete your account go to your Profile page and use the Delete Account option. This will permanently remove your account and data.

7. Cookies

Inkwell uses Firebase Authentication which may store authentication tokens in your browser's local storage to keep you logged in between visits. These are not used for advertising or tracking purposes.

8. Children's Privacy

Inkwell is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
